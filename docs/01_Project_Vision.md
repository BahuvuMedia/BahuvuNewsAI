\# BahuvuNewsAI - Project Vision



\*\*Document Version:\*\* 1.0  

\*\*Project:\*\* BahuvuNewsAI  

\*\*Owner:\*\* BAHUVU News  

\*\*Purpose:\*\* AI-powered news research, production, and publishing system  

\*\*Status:\*\* Architecture Foundation  



\---



\## 1. Project Mission



BahuvuNewsAI is being developed to create a professional AI-powered newsroom system for BAHUVU News.



The mission of the project is to automate and assist the complete news production workflow, including:



\- news collection,

\- article cleaning,

\- editorial validation,

\- summarization,

\- Telugu translation,

\- headline generation,

\- broadcast graphics,

\- narration,

\- video composition,

\- thumbnail creation,

\- metadata preparation,

\- and YouTube publishing support.



The system is designed to help produce publishable news content with consistency, speed, accuracy, and professional presentation quality.



\---



\## 2. Long-Term Vision



The long-term vision of BahuvuNewsAI is to become an autonomous digital newsroom assistant that can support monthly, weekly, and eventually daily news production.



The system should be capable of:



1\. collecting important news from reliable sources,

2\. selecting high-value stories,

3\. cleaning and validating article content,

4\. preparing Telugu news scripts,

5\. generating professional graphics,

6\. producing narration,

7\. composing news videos,

8\. creating thumbnails and metadata,

9\. preparing uploads for YouTube,

10\. and maintaining a structured archive of produced content.



The final goal is not just to create scripts or images, but to build a complete production pipeline that supports real publishing.



\---



\## 3. Primary Use Case



The primary use case is monthly news production for BAHUVU News.



The planned publishing model is:



\- one monthly compilation video,

\- released on the 1st day of every month,

\- covering important news from the previous month,

\- prepared in a professional Telugu news format,

\- optimized for YouTube publishing.



Future versions may support weekly or daily production once the monthly pipeline becomes stable.



\---



\## 4. Target Audience



The target audience includes:



\- Telugu-speaking viewers,

\- Indian news audiences,

\- viewers interested in public affairs, governance, weather, society, technology, economy, and important current events,

\- YouTube news viewers who prefer clear, concise, and visually professional news summaries.



The system should help create content that is understandable, trustworthy, and visually suitable for public publishing.



\---



\## 5. Core Goals



BahuvuNewsAI has the following core goals:



\### 5.1 Editorial Quality



The system must produce content that is:



\- clear,

\- accurate,

\- relevant,

\- cleanly written,

\- free from junk text,

\- suitable for translation,

\- and appropriate for public news publishing.



\### 5.2 Telugu News Production



The system must support Telugu news creation, including:



\- Telugu translation,

\- Telugu script formatting,

\- Telugu narration readiness,

\- and Telugu-friendly visual layout.



\### 5.3 Professional Broadcast Presentation



The system must generate visual output that looks suitable for a news channel, including:



\- branded layouts,

\- consistent typography,

\- readable headlines,

\- clean image placement,

\- category labels,

\- lower thirds,

\- thumbnails,

\- and video-ready graphics.



\### 5.4 Automation



The system should reduce repetitive manual work by automating:



\- article selection,

\- cleaning,

\- validation,

\- formatting,

\- graphics generation,

\- narration preparation,

\- video assembly,

\- and publishing preparation.



\### 5.5 Maintainability



The system must be built in a way that is easy to extend, test, debug, and maintain.



Every module should have a clear responsibility.



\---



\## 6. Non-Goals



The current project does not aim to immediately become a fully independent newsroom without human review.



At the current stage, BahuvuNewsAI should not:



\- publish automatically without human approval,

\- generate unsupported facts,

\- ignore source quality,

\- bypass editorial validation,

\- produce misleading content,

\- or replace final human editorial judgment.



Human review remains important before public publishing.



\---



\## 7. Guiding Principles



The following principles guide all future development.



\### 7.1 Architecture First



Design decisions should be made before code is written.



Every new file must fit into the documented architecture.



\### 7.2 One Responsibility Per Module



Each Python file should perform one clear job.



A module should not mix unrelated responsibilities.



\### 7.3 Stable Interfaces



Modules should communicate through clear inputs and outputs.



Once a module is stable, other modules should depend on its public behavior, not its internal code.



\### 7.4 Test Before Commit



Every completed module must be:



1\. compiled,

2\. tested,

3\. committed,

4\. pushed,

5\. and considered stable.



\### 7.5 Human-in-the-Loop Publishing



Final publishing decisions should remain under human control.



The AI system assists production, but does not remove editorial responsibility.



\### 7.6 Telugu-First Readiness



Since Telugu news production is a major goal, all text, layout, translation, and narration modules should be designed with Telugu support in mind.



\### 7.7 Professional Output



The system should aim for publishable quality, not only experimental output.



\---



\## 8. Success Criteria



BahuvuNewsAI will be considered successful when it can reliably produce a complete monthly news package containing:



\- selected important news stories,

\- cleaned and validated article text,

\- Telugu-ready scripts,

\- professional graphics,

\- narration-ready content,

\- video-ready assets,

\- thumbnail-ready output,

\- YouTube title and description,

\- and a structured archive of the package.



A future complete success state will include:



\- one-command monthly package generation,

\- clear review points,

\- export-ready videos,

\- and publishing preparation.
