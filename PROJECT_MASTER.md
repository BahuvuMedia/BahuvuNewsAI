\# PROJECT VISION



BahuvuNewsAI is an AI-powered newsroom platform designed to automate the complete news production workflow—from collecting stories to publishing professional broadcast-quality news videos.



Primary objectives:



\- Automated news collection

\- Editorial intelligence

\- Fact verification

\- Professional script generation

\- Telugu translation

\- AI voice narration

\- Broadcast graphics

\- Video composition

\- YouTube publishing

\- Monitoring and analytics
